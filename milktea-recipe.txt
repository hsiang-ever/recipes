Ingredients:
 - 2 orange pekoe tea bags
 - 1 cup boiling water
 - 5 ice cubes
 - 4 teaspoons sweetened condensed milk
 - 3 teaspoons honey

Directions:
 1. Steep the tea bags in hot water until the color turns dark red, about 3 to 5 minutes. Discard the 	   tea bags and let the tea cool.

 2. Combine the ice cubes, sweetened condensed milk, and honey in a glass or cocktail shaker. Pour in 	   the tea and mix well. (If the tea is still warm, the ice may melt; add more ice if desired.)
 	A strong, flavorful milk tea is ready for you to enjoy.
